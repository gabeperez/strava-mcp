# 🏃 Strava MCP - Share Safely!

## 🔐 **Privacy Guaranteed**

**YES - You can safely share this MCP URL with anyone:**

```
https://your-worker-name.your-subdomain.workers.dev/mcp
```

### **Your Data is 100% Private Because:**
- ✅ Each person must authenticate with **their own** Strava account
- ✅ Your Strava data is tied to **your session** only  
- ✅ Sessions are isolated per user
- ✅ No one else can access your activities, routes, or stats

Think of it like sharing a Gmail login page - everyone uses the same URL, but sees only their own data.

---

## 🚀 **Setup Instructions for Others**

When someone uses your MCP URL, here's what happens:

### **1. First Connection**
- They add the MCP URL to their AI assistant (Poke, Claude, etc.)
- The AI will automatically prompt them to authenticate
- They click the Strava OAuth link
- They log in with **their own** Strava account

### **2. Ready to Use!**
After authentication, they can ask natural language queries like:
- *"Show me my recent Strava activities"*
- *"What was my heart rate data from my last run?"*  
- *"Find challenging climbs near Boulder, Colorado"*

## 🛡️ **Security Features**

- **Per-User Authentication**: Each person connects their own account
- **Session Isolation**: Your data never mixes with theirs
- **Automatic Token Refresh**: Sessions stay secure
- **No Data Storage**: No activity data is permanently stored
- **OAuth Security**: Uses Strava's official authentication
